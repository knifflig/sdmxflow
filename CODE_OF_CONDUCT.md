# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a harassment-free experience for everyone.
We commit to making participation welcoming, inclusive, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on the technical discussion.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Help create a positive environment for others.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainer via GitHub.
The fastest path is to contact the repository owner (see the Contact section in the README).

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and/or temporarily or permanently ban
contributors who engage in behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
